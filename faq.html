INTEGRA CO-OP is a modern cooperative society designed to help you save, invest, and grow wealth ethically. We leverage the power of community to unlock financial opportunities, offering structured savings, low-interest loans, and vetted investment portfolios.

Joining is easy! Simply click the "Join Now" button on our website to create an account. You will need to complete a brief registration form and pay a one-time membership fee to activate your account and start enjoying our benefits.

Members become eligible for loans after a qualifying period of consistent savings (usually 3-6 months). You can apply directly through your dashboard. Our loans come with low interest rates and flexible repayment plans designed to support your growth.

For most personal and small business loans, your savings and the guarantee of fellow members serve as collateral, meaning you don't need physical assets. Larger facility requests may require additional documentation.

We offer pooled investment opportunities in sectors such as Real Estate, Agriculture, and Money Market instruments. These are vetted by our financial experts to ensure they yield competitive returns above inflation rates.
